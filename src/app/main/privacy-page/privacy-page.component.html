Privacy Policy

Last updated on 12th December 2019

This privacy policy only refers to what is running on Elypia's servers regardless of what website or service you're using. If you're connected to a third-party host; you should read the respective privacy policy for your host.

None of our websites or services store personal information on users, and our web servers are configured with the access log disabled, so we do not see information regarding your request such as IP or user-agent.

We're using CloudFlare as a Content Delivery Network (CDN) which collects and allows us to view aggregate data on traffic such as total requests, number of unique visitors, and which countries are predominantly accessing our services.

If there are any concerns or questions regarding our privacy policy, or if you have a request, please send an email to dpo@elypia.org where the elected Data Protection Officer (DPO) will be able to support you.

ePrivacy Directive

The ePrivacy directive, also known as the EU Cookie Law, is what covers the use of cookies (or other client-side storage mediums) and consent. We minimize the use of cookies and storage without reducing functionality on the websites or services. We do not, and most likely never will, use or add analytic cookies, or trackers, however we do store some data for technical or client-side configuration purposes.

Where third-party scripts is embedded from other websites, third-party cookies may also be added.
