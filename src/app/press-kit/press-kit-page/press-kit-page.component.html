This is for users or publishers that may want to reference Elypia and use its branding. This page covers some assets that can be used, and any guidelines to using them.

Guidelines

  • Keeping the logo as-is is preferred, but you may alter the hue.
  • Other than as explicitly stated, don't modify or reconfigure the Elypia logo.
  • Don't use the Elypia branding where it may suggest we endorse your content.
