Press Kit

This press kit is dedicated to users or publishers that may want to publish anything that references Elypia and wants to use it's branding. This page covers some assets that can be used, and any guidelines to using them.

Guidelines

  • It's best to keep the logo as true as possible, but you may alter the hue/saturation.
  • Other than as explicitly stated, don't modify or reconfigure the Elypia logo.
  • Don't use Elypia's branding where it suggests Elypia endorses your content.
